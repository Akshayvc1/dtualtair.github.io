Robo Rally

Specifications

    Wheels: Standard 10cm plastic wheels with added rubber grip
    Motors: 12V 150 RPM high torque DC motor
    Motor Driver: L298N
    Material Used: Metal chassis and Wooden board
    Battery Type: 2200mAh LiPo
    Microcontroller: Arduino Mega

Description

The basic structure of the robot consists of a metal chassis with a wooden board on top supported by four long bolts. All the electronics was sandwiched in between the chassis and wooden roof in order to protect it. The bot consists of four wheels with special rubber grip for better traction. High torque motors were used to provide drive in all four wheels to ensure that the bot navigates even the most difficult terrain with ease. The robot was designed with adequate ground clearance to maneuver rocky terrain effortlessly. Two L298N motor drivers were used to split the load of DC motors and provide drive in all four wheels. The robot was controlled wirelessly by a PlayStation controller which was configured using the PS2X library of Arduino. The communication between the transmitter and receiver was established using the PWM functionality of Arduino to receive digital as well as analog signals.
