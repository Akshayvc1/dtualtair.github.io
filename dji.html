DJI F-450

Specifications

    Motors: 2200KV BLDC
    ESC: 30A DYS
    Propellers: 10 Inch
    Flight control board: DJI Naza-M Lite
    Receiver Transmitter: FlySky FS-i6
    Battery: 3600mAh LiPo

Description

It is Altair's first and most powerful drone possessing the ability to travel at high speeds and lift payloads of up to 5 Kgs for transportation. Equipped with GPS and failsafe mode, this drone lands itself safely upon losing connection with the transmitter. Mounting a high definition camera or simply strapping a smartphone to its base gives it the ability to capture stunning videos and photographs. Initially designed for speed and agility, it is one of the most versatile drones which has proved its utility, both for research and competitions. With slight modifications, it can be easily converted into an autonomous drone utilizing AI to map and document complex terrain.
